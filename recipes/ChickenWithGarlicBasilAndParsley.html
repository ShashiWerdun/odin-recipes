Chicken with Garlic, Basil, and Parsley

Description

Baked chicken seasoned with garlic, parsley, basil, tomato, and red pepper flakes. Simple dinner. This dish has a prep time of around 10 minutes and cooking time of 40 minutes, totalling to 50 minutes. For the given ingredients the dish offers 4 servings.

Nutrition content of this dish: 150 calories; protein 25.6g; carbohydrates 4.1g; fat 3.1g; cholesterol 67.2mg; sodium 354.7mg.

Ingredients

  • Dried Parsley, divided: 1 tablespoon
  • Dried Basil ,divided: 1 tablespoon
  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast halves: 4
  • Garlic cloves, sliced thin: 4
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Crushed Red pepper flakes: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sliced tomatoes: 2

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Coat a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Sprinkle 1 teaspoon parsley and 1 teaspoon basil evenly over the bottom of the baking dish.
  4. Arrange chicken breast halves in the dish, and sprinkle evenly with garlic slices.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the remaining 2 teaspoons parsley, remaining 2 teaspoons basil, salt, and red pepper; sprinkle over the chicken.
  6. Top with tomato slices.
  7. Bake covered in the preheated oven 25 minutes.
  8. Remove cover, and continue baking 15 minutes, or until chicken juices run clear.
